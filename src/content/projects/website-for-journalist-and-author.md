Muiris Houston is a well known medical journalist, writer and Health Strategist - he has written weekly for the Irish Times om health matters for over 20 years.

Muiris approached us in advance of the release of his biography on the renowned Irish psychiatrist, writer and broadcaster Anthony Clare - he needed a website to help publicize the launch and provide background information on his interests and writings. Our remit from Muiris was

 	Rework of logo

 	Design, Typography, Colour Palette and graphics on the site

 	Development of the website and CMS

 	Promotion of the book.

Our process to execute this project

We employed WordPress for the website CMS and provided training. The website features

 	The ability to amend content from the front end

 	Add news items / articles of interest

 	Bio information on the author

 	Information about the book and how to purchase

A very simple design layout and typography was employed and a bespoke build undertaken. Our goal was to make the site load as quickly as possible and provide the owner with the ability to control the site content with as much ease as possible.
