Aisling McEntee Walsh is an artist based in Dublin, Ireland. Aisling wanted a website to showcase her work, importantly she wanted to be able to access and keep the site content updated without the need for intervention from her web developer.

For an image-rich website it's extra important to develop the site with speed in mind. We knew the design needed to be minimal and clean allowing the artist's work to speak for itself.

We devised a typography guide and reworked Aisling's logo.
A clean design was chosen and developed, the CMS was adapted to Aisling's explicit needs around control of galleries, sorting of images, titles and captions.
Gallery design and function.
Support and maintenance.
