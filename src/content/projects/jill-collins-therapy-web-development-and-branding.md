Project Overview:

 Jill Collins is a dedicated therapist with 13 years of experience, driven by a passion for holistic health. With a diverse skillset, she employs various therapeutic approaches to help clients achieve optimal well-being. 

Her main services focus on Craniosacral Therapy (CST) for babies and children. Collaborating closely with Jill, we undertook a comprehensive project to revamp her brand and build a new, user-friendly website.

 
 Key Contributions:

 
 Logo and Brand Redevelopment:
 
 Worked with Kay McKeon at Patterns.ie to redesign Jill's overall brand identity, ensuring it reflected her holistic approach and professional ethos.

 
 

 Photography Organization:
 
 Organized professional photography sessions to capture high-quality images for use on the website, enhancing visual appeal and client engagement.

 
 

 Website Design and Development:
 
 Designed and developed a modern, responsive WordPress website tailored to Jill's therapeutic services and brand identity.

 Integrated design elements to ensure a cohesive and inviting online presence.

 
 

 Booking Tool Integration:
 
 Integrated a booking tool to streamline appointment scheduling, improving operational efficiency and client convenience.

 Provided training and ongoing support to ensure effective use of the booking system.

 
 

 
 
 Outcome:

 The holistic approach taken in redeveloping Jill Collins Therapy's brand and online presence has significantly enhanced client engagement and streamlined appointment scheduling. This project highlights our expertise in delivering comprehensive web development and branding solutions tailored to the unique needs of holistic health professionals.
