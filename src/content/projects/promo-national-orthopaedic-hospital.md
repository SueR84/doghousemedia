Project Overview

In 2024 we had the privilege of creating a 4-minute promotional video for the National Orthopaedic Hospital, Cappagh in Dublin. This video was showcased at their flagship fundraising event in Dublin in spring 2024, a lunch event that gathered key stakeholders and supporters.

Project Details

Our video featured in-depth interviews with the CEO of the hospital, the Clinical Director, and two patients who shared their personal experiences and testimonials. To provide a comprehensive view of the hospital’s facilities, we captured detailed footage of various operational areas including the operating rooms, recovery areas, and physiotherapy rooms.
