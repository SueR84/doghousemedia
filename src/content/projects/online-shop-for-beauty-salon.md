This was a hugely rewarding project - launching the website and online shop for a boutique salon in the centre of Kilkenny. We advised the client on

 	Logo design, branding (through our design parnter friends at Patterns)

 	Website Design - a bespoke design was developed

CMS - WordPress. A bespoke design was developed and implemented.

 	E-Commerce - full integration with Shopify

The website was built using a bespoke design and theme based on branding guidelines from the designer. 

Shopify products and checkout are fully integrated with the website, meaning the client can make full use of the Shopify app to manage products and orders. 

Taking it further

We advised the client on how to add more visibility to the brand and a plan was implemented to get the Google Business Profile optimised and increase local searches from Google. 
Local map pack rankings have increased dramatically in the 6 months since go-live.
