Shapes of Grief is an award winning Grief Therapy Course and Podcast. The Course was formerly delivered on Squarespace and the client wanted to migrate this to a more robust and powerful LMS solution.

In addition the client took the opportunity to re-brand. We worked with Kay at Patterns to a new website layout and design to go with the new branding. Colour Palette, Typography and graphics were married with a bespoke design.
Learning Management System

We transferred the entire course and participants to a new LMS, self-hosted on WordPress. This gave us the full functionality and flexibility of WordPress, married with the capabilities of an online course delivered via an LMS. Course features include

 	Course registration and online payment

 	Intuitive Course builder

 	Course certificates

 	Student tracking

 	Coupons

 	Email Marketing integration

 Abandoned Cart Funnels 

 Upsells and course bundling
