Merrion Medical Aesthetics are an advanced skincare Clinic in Toronto offering services like Botox and skin rejuvenation. 
 
 Project Overview:

 Working collaboratively with Merrion Medical Aesthetics and their graphic designer, we embarked on a comprehensive project to build a new, user-friendly website on the WordPress platform. This project encompassed several key components to enhance the client's digital presence and operational efficiency.

 
 Key Contributions:

 
 Website Development:
 
 Developed a modern, responsive WordPress website tailored to the aesthetic and functional requirements set forth by Merrion Medical Aesthetics.

 Ensured seamless integration of the design elements provided by their graphic designer, maintaining brand consistency and visual appeal.

 
 

 Booking System Integration:
 
 Integrated a robust booking system to streamline appointment scheduling for clients, enhancing user experience and operational workflow.

 Provided support to the Merrion team to maximize the system's utility.

 
 

 CRM System Integration:
 
 Advised on the selection and implementation of a CRM system to better manage client relationships and data.

 Integrated the CRM system with the website, ensuring smooth data flow and accessibility.

 
 

 E-Commerce Shop Development:
 
 We built an e-commerce shop using Shopify, integrating it seamlessly with their WordPress websit

 Enabled Merrion Medical Aesthetics to manage and sell their products online efficiently, with a focus on user experience and security.

 
 

 SEO Advisory:
 
 Provided strategic SEO advice to improve the website's visibility on search engines.

 Implemented SEO best practices during the website build to ensure long-term benefits in organic search rankings.

 
 

 
 
 Outcome:

 The comprehensive solution provided to Merrion Medical Aesthetics has significantly enhanced their online presence, streamlined their operations, and enabled efficient online sales
